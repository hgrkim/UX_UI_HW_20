What's the

Problem?

Travelers are overwhelmed when planning for trips because all their resources are scattered and they do not have the most accurate information.

What's the

Solution?

MYTRIP, a trip planning app that travelers can use to store travel documents/tickets, create an itinerary, receive receive activity updates, and collaborate with friends, all in one app.
